DIRECTIONS TO PREMISES

Travelling from Dublin, Cork, Waterford, Limerick on the N20 exit to Blarney you are then on the R617 on reaching the edge of Blarney Village you will pass two Petrol stations ( one each side ) take the second right, direction waterloop  after approx. 500m you will see our B+B sign turn left up the lane, after 100m you are there!!

Travelling from West Cork & Kerry, as you enter the village take a left onto the relief road, Blarney Medical Centre is on your left and Malones Pub is on your right. After you go over the brow of the hill you will see a turn to the left for waterloop, turn left after approx. 500m you will see our B+B sign turn left up the lane, after 100m you are there!!
